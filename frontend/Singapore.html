Frequently Asked
Questions

Unlock the answers to your queries with our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)!

  • What is the food like in Singapore?

    • Wide availability of diverse cuisines including Indian, Malay, Chinese, and Western options • Numerous Indian grocery stores and restaurants catering to South Asian students • Universities offer vegetarian, halal, and special diet-friendly meals • Food courts and hawker centers provide affordable, authentic local dining experiences

  • Travel & Transportation

    • Student Pass required for full-time study exceeding 30 days, applied via SOLAR system • Direct flights connect India with Singapore Changi Airport, a global transport hub • Efficient public transport system includes MRT (metro), buses, and taxis with student concession cards available • Compact city-state design allows easy commuting and access to university campuses

  • What is included in the housing facilities?

    • Options include university hostels, private student residences, condominiums, and HDB flats • University accommodations provide secure, convenient living, often with social and academic support • Private housing offers flexibility but can be more expensive and requires lease understanding • Many students share rental spaces to reduce cost

  • What languages do people use in Singapore?

    • English is the main language of instruction and daily communication • Mandarin, Malay, and Tamil are also official languages, contributing to a multicultural environment • Language support and cultural orientation programs are available for international students • Multilingual campus communities foster cultural exchange and inclusivity

  • Why Choose Singapore for Higher Education?

    • Home to globally ranked universities with strong research and innovation focus • Safe, clean, and politically stable city-state with excellent infrastructure • Strong ties with industry and high employability for graduates • Multicultural society offering rich cultural experiences and global networking • Access to scholarships, grants, and financial aid for international students

  • How does the education system function in Singapore?

    • Comprises polytechnics, universities, and specialized institutes with a focus on practical skills • Academic year generally starts in August with multiple intakes available • Emphasis on technology, business, engineering, life sciences, and arts • Degrees recognized globally with emphasis on innovation and industry readiness

  • What is the climate like in Singapore?

    • Tropical rainforest climate with warm and humid weather year-round • Temperatures average 25-31°C with frequent afternoon showers • Students should be prepared for humidity and occasional heavy rains

  • Stay Back & Future Opportunities

    • Student Pass holders may work part-time up to 16 hours per week during term • Post-study work visas available under schemes like the Professional Conversion Program. • Strong job market in finance, technology, biomedical sciences, and logistics • Graduates benefit from Singapore’s status as a global business and innovation hub

  • What is the climate like in Singapore Currency?

    • Official currency is the Singapore Dollar (SGD) • As of October 2025, 1 Indian Rupee (INR) ≈ 0.017 SGD • Familiarity with SGD and budgeting is essential for living expenses management

  • How would you describe the Culture of Singapore?

    • Diverse cultural fabric blending Chinese, Malay, Indian, and Western influences • Numerous festivals, community events, and student organizations celebrate multiculturalism • Respect, discipline, and academic excellence are valued societal traits • Vibrant arts, food, and entertainment scenes provide rich social experiences.

  • How is the Survivability in Singapore?

    • Exceptional public safety with stringent laws and low crime rates • World-class healthcare facilities accessible through universities and public systems • Universities offer counseling, orientation, and comprehensive student support services • Efficient urban infrastructure ensures comfortable and convenient living
